## Context

High digital-banking adoption concentrates financial and personal data on mobile
and payment platforms. Policy must balance innovation with **security, privacy,
and consumer trust**.

## Planned recommendation areas

1. **Consumer Protection** — grievance resolution SLAs and public metrics
2. **Data Privacy** — plain-language consent and privacy notices
3. **Payments Security** — fraud alerts, MFA, and transaction limits
4. **Digital Governance** — independent ombudsman reporting
5. **Financial Literacy** — cybersecurity awareness for young users
